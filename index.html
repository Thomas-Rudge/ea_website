Established in September 2000, Entrepreneurs Associates (EA) is an organization of First Generation Entrepreneurs (FGEs) passionately committed to developing entrepreneurship and sustainable livelihoods in Nagaland for economic development, creation of jobs, social justice, and increased participation of local people in the economic activities of the state for social equity and harmony. The organization works directly with people to empower communities and individuals through sustainable social entrepreneurship programs.
